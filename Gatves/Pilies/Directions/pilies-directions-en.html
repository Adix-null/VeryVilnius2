Directions With parking

Directions

Location

The address is Pilies str. 38, Vilnius. The entry is through a gateway.

Obtaining the keys

When You walk up to the postboxes, You will see a keybox on your right. Open the upper keybox with a code you received through SMS. When You have taken the keys from the keybox, close it and dial a random combination, do not leave it unlocked.

Entering the apartment

Now you can proceed to the brown door and unlock it. When you have climed up the stairs, you will see a walkway, in the end of which there will be a white door with a number 23 on it, which is the apartment.

With parking

Entering the courtyard

The entry for cars to the courtyard is from another street - Latako str. 2. This is a narrow one-way street, so do not hurry and make a right turn after You pass the yellow building. Here You will see the gate to the courtyard parking area.

Opening the gate

Near the gate on your left there is a keybox on the wall. When you have dialed the code which you received, a box will open and you will find a remote for the gate. Push the button "C" to open the gate.
When You have taken the keys from the keybox, close it and dial a random combination, do not leave it unlocked. Then, when You have parked your car, go on top of the incline, then turn to Your left and go through an arch.

Obtaining the keys

When You walk up to the postboxes, You will see a keybox on your left. Open the keybox with a code you received through SMS. When You have taken the keys from the keybox, close it and dial a random combination, do not leave it unlocked. Now you can proceed to the brown door and unlock it.

Entering the apartment

When you have entered the building and climed up the stairs, you will see a white door on the left with number 23 on it, which is the apartment.
