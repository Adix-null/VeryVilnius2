Directions

Location

The address is Pilies str. 38, Vilnius. The entry is through a gateway.

Obtaining the keys

When You walk up to the postboxes, You will see a keybox on your right. Open the upper keybox with a code you received through SMS. When You have taken the keys from the keybox, close it and dial a random combination, do not leave it unlocked.

Entering the apartment

Now you can proceed to the brown door and unlock it. When you have climed up the stairs, you will see a walkway, in the end of which there will be a white door with a number 23 on it, which is the apartment.
