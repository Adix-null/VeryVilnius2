Information

Pilies street apartment

Enterance

You will receive information about where the keys will be left the day before arrival.

Please note: the apartment is in the attic of an Old Town building, so the staircase and apartment have spiral stairs.

Wifi

Network: EXTERNUS

Password: veryvilnius.com

TV Control

Turn on both remotes, press the Home button on the small remote.

Use the small remote to select TV Guide at the top of the screen.

If you use your own device with the TV, please reconnect all cables as they were when you arrived.

Water

Tap water is drinkable.

Heating/Cooling

The apartment has autonomous gas underfloor heating. You can adjust the heat using the dial on the wall between the railing and the bathroom, or in the bedroom. Please do not adjust the control screen, as it’s set to prioritize bathroom floor heating. Increasing room temperature will reduce bathroom floor warmth. Contact the host if needed.

The fireplace can be used; for better airflow, open the window before lighting it.

Air conditioners are in the living and bedroom areas. In summer, set it to cooling (snowflake symbol); in winter, set it to heating (sun symbol) if needed.

Smoking

Smoking, including electronic cigarettes, is strictly prohibited inside. Fines apply. Use the balcony in the staircase (white door near the semi-circular window).

Trash

Trash containers are at the end of the inner courtyard. Exit the staircase and turn right.

Equipment

The apartment includes: electric stove, oven, microwave, dishwasher, washing machine, Nespresso capsule coffee machine, electric kettle.

Departure

Before leaving, turn off any additional appliances (bathroom towel heater, air conditioner).

Check-out by 11:00 AM. Lock the apartment and leave the keys in the same box.

Take out the trash to the outdoor container.

Please send message to host, we can better organize cleaning time.
