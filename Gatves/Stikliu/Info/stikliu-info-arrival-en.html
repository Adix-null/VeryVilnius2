Information

Entrance

You will receive information about the entrance code, apartment key, and parking remote the day before arrival.

The stairwell door closes automatically, but if you have more belongings, you can fix the door open by pushing it past a 90-degree angle to make carrying your things easier.

WiFi

Username: Externus1

Password: veryvilnius.com

TV Control

Turn on both remotes.

Select "TV" on the top of the screen (with the small remote).

If you use your own equipment to connect to the TV, please reconnect all cables as they were when you arrived.

Water

Tap water is drinkable.

Hot water is heated by a boiler. If there are multiple guests, please avoid using the shower simultaneously to ensure there’s enough hot water for everyone.

Heating/Cooling

The apartment has central heating, which cannot be adjusted.

For additional heating, you can turn on the heated floors in the bathroom and kitchen. The controller is located near the floor.

In the living room between the windows, there’s an electric radiator.

An additional portable electric radiator can be found in the storage room between the bedrooms.

We are unable to install an air conditioner, so during summer, please use the provided fan.

Sofa Bed in the Living Room

The sofa easily converts into a comfortable bed. Simply remove the backrest cushions, lift the seating part upward and toward you, and unfold the backrest. After unzipping the compartment on the back, spread out the thin mattress cover across the entire bed. Place the backrest cushions back against the wall.

Safe

You can use the safe. Please contact the hosts for the code.

Smoking

Smoking is not allowed inside the apartment or stairwell. Please use the outdoor courtyard for smoking.

Trash

The trash container is located 200 m away from the apartment, heading toward Vokiečių Street via Antokolskio Street. It is locked. The code is 1569 — press one number at a time, not all at once.

Equipment

The apartment includes a washing machine, dishwasher, gas stove (operated by push-button ignition without matches), oven, microwave, and a Dolce Gusto coffee capsule machine.

In the main bedroom, for better sleep, you can use the blackout blinds on the windows. During winter, you may use the blinds alone without closing the curtains to ensure the heat from the radiator is not blocked.

Departure

Leave all keys in the same place where you found them (don’t forget to leave the additional key set in the apartment).

Turn off all extra electrical devices (electric radiators and heated floors).

Take out the trash to the outdoor container.

There is a code lock at the courtyard gate. The pedestrian gate remains open during the day and closes automatically in the evening.

It can be opened with a code or a magnetic key fob.

The main parking gate opens by pressing the letter "A" on the remote control.

The stairwell door also has a code lock, opened with the same code or magnetic key fob.

You will receive information about the entrance code, apartment key location, and parking remote the day before arrival.
