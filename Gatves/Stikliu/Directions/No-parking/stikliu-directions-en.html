Directions

Location

The address is Gaono str. 10, flat no. 2, Vilnius. The entry is through a gateway. If the gates to the yard are closed, please enter the code 2*3909 on the wall to Your left.

Obtaining the keys

When You go through the gate, You will see a keybox on your right. Open the ABUS keybox with a code you have received. When You have taken the apartment keys from the keybox, close it and dial a random combination, do not leave it unlocked.

Going inside

Now you can go to the brown door and enter the same code 2*3909 on the left wall . Then turn right and climb up the stairs.

Entering the apartment

You will be able to open Apartment number 2 with the key you have gotten outside.
