Directions

Location

The address is Gaono str. 10, flat no. 2, Vilnius.

The entry is through a gateway. If the gates to the yard are closed, please enter the code 2*3909 on the wall to Your left.

Obtaining the keys

When You go through the gate, You will see a keybox on your right. Open the ABUS keybox with a code you have received in the email.

In the keybox You will find the apartment key and a remote for the gate, if parking is necessary. The 'A' button on the remote opens the gates, who automatically close after 30 sec.

When You have taken the apartment keys from the keybox, close it and dial a random combination, do not leave it unlocked.

Going inside

Now you can go to the brown door and enter the same code 2*3909 on the left wall . Then turn right and climb up the stairs.

Entering the apartment

You will be able to open Apartment number 2 with the key you have gotten outside.

Exit / Departure

When leaving, open the pedestrian gate by pressing the "Exit" button on the walls near the gate.
On your departure, please leave the apartment key and the remote control in the same box.

You can also use the intercom during your stay (person located outside presses no. 2 on the gate and stairwell door, and the one in the apartment lets in).
