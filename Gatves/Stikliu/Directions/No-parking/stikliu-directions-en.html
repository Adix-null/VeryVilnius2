Directions

Location

The address is Gaono str. 10, Vilnius. The entry is through a gateway.

Obtaining the keys

When You go through the gate, You will see a keybox on your right. Open the keybox with a code you received through SMS. When You have taken the keys from the keybox, close it and dial a random combination, do not leave it unlocked.

Going inside

Now you can go to the brown door and unlock it, turn right and climb up the stairs.

Entering the apartment

When you have entered the building and climed up the stairs, the door with a number 2 on it will be the apartment.
