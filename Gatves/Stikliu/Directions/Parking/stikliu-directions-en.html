Directions with parking

Parking the car

The entry for cars to the courtyard is from another street - Latako str. 2. This is a narrow one-way street, so do not hurry and make a right turn after You pass the yellow building. Here You will see the gate to the courtyard parking area.

Opening the gate

Near the gate on your left there is a keybox on the wall. When you have dialed the code which you received, a box will open and you will find a remote for the gate. Push the button "C" to open the gate. When You have taken the keys from the keybox, close it and dial a random combination, do not leave it unlocked.

Entering the courtyard

When you have parked your car return to Latako str., go up the hill and through the park to Gaono str. 10. how it is shown in the map.

Obtaining the keys

When You go through the gate, You will see a keybox on your right. Open the keybox with a code you received through SMS. When You have taken the keys from the keybox, close it and dial a random combination, do not leave it unlocked.

Going inside

Now you can go to the brown door and unlock it, turn right and climb up the stairs.

Entering the apartment

When you have climbed up the stairs, the door with a number 2 on it will be the apartment.
