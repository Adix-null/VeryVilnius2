Directions

The address is Vilniaus g. 25, suite 32, Vilnius. The entry is through the big grey door.

Outside in the right side of the door please enter the code 32*3663.

Go up the stairs.

On the right you will find a keybox on the wall.

Enter the code you have received and when You have taken the keys from the keybox, close it and dial a random combination, do not leave it unlocked.

Enter through the white door and in the corridor on the right side will be apartment no. 32.
The key locks apartment and corridor doors, and the black round key is used from the outside instead of the code.

Upon leaving the building, press the "EXIT" button on the left side of the door.
