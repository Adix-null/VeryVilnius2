Information

Vilnius street apartment

Enterance

You will receive information about where the keys will be left the day before arrival.

Building doors are open during working hours on weekdays. In the evening and on weekends, they close automatically. To exit, press the EXIT button on the wall to the left of the door.

Wifi

Network: EXTERNUS

Password: veryvilnius.com

TV Control

Turn on both remotes, press the Home button on the small remote.

Use the small remote to select TV Guide at the top of the screen.

If you use your own equipment to connect to the TV, please reconnect all cables as they were when you arrived.

Water

Tap water is drinkable.

Heating/Cooling

The apartment has central heating; radiators under the windows can be adjusted.

No air conditioning is available. Use the fan found in the closet by the bed. If using it with water, the air will be cooler and cleaner than just using the fan alone. Don’t forget to check the water tank.

Noise cancelling

The apartment is located in the city center near bars and restaurants, so some noise is expected, especially on weekends. If you are sensitive to noise, we recommend using earplugs, which can be found in the bedside table drawer.

Safe

You can use the safe. Please contact the hosts for the code.

  • To create a new code, press the CLEAR button twice and you will see the message NEW. You can now enter a new code (between 3 and 9 digits).
  • When you have entered your new code, press the LOCK button and you will see the message AGAIN. Re-enter the code and press LOCK once more. You will then see the message DONE. Your new code is now active.
  • To lock the safe, close the door and press the LOCK button. (MAKE SURE THAT YOU REMEMBER YOUR CODE BEFORE LOCKING THE SAFE!)
  • To open the safe, simply enter your code and the door will open. In case You forgot safe code – please call owner. ATTENTION: PLEASE DO NOT FORGET YOUR BELONGINGS IN THE SAFE.

Disclaimer: Safe box provided to use for You convenience as You decide. We as owners can not accept any responsibility for Your belongings

Trash

The locked container is in the courtyard. Exit the staircase, turn right, enter the first archway, and the containers will be immediately on the left behind a lock. The key is on the shelf near the apartment door.

Equipment

The apartment includes: electric stove, oven, microwave, dishwasher, washing machine, electric kettle.

Keys

The apartment key also opens the white door in the corridor, in case someone locks it.

Departure

Before leaving, turn off all additional appliances (like the bathroom towel heater).

Check-out by 12:00 PM. Lock the apartment and leave the keys in the same box, spinning any numbers to lock it.

Take out the trash to the outdoor container.

Please send message to host, we can better organize cleaning time.
